**Cosmicus** is a gamified application designed to teach computational thinking in a fun and engaging way. With a space-themed adventure, players learn core problem-solving skills through interactive challenges and missions.

## Features

- **Gamified Learning**: Solve puzzles and complete missions to advance through space.
- **Core Computational Concepts**: Learn sequencing, pattern recognition, abstraction, and algorithmic thinking.
- **Engaging Challenges**: Explore different planets, each representing a computational concept.
- **Interactive Storyline**: Experience an immersive space journey while mastering problem-solving skills.
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor learning progress and unlock new levels.

## How to Play

1. Start your mission by solving space-themed computational puzzles.
2. Earn rewards and progress through different planets.
3. Level up and become a master of computational thinking!

## Learning Objectives

Cosmicus aims to enhance:

- **Logical Reasoning**
- **Problem-Solving Skills**
- **Pattern Recognition**
- **Decomposition & Abstraction**
